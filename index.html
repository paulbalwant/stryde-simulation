üìö Leadership Communication Handbook

Core Principles for This Simulation:

1. Emotional Intelligence (EI)

  • Self-Awareness: Recognize your own communication style and biases
  • Self-Regulation: Control emotional responses in high-pressure situations
  • Empathy: Understand team members' perspectives and concerns
  • Social Skills: Build relationships and influence effectively

2. Persuasion & Influence

  • Reciprocity: Give what you want to receive
  • Liking: Build rapport through genuine connection
  • Social Proof: Leverage peer influence
  • Authority: Establish credibility and expertise
  • Consistency: Align actions with commitments
  • Scarcity: Highlight unique value and urgency

3. Effective Written Communication

  • CPF Structure: Context, Purpose, Foreshadowing
  • Clarity: Use concrete, specific language
  • Tone: Match formality to audience and situation
  • Brevity: Respect your reader's time (50-150 words ideal)

4. Inclusive Leadership

  • Visible Commitment: Actively champion diversity and inclusion
  • Humility: Acknowledge your limitations and learn from others
  • Curiosity: Seek to understand different perspectives
  • Cultural Intelligence: Adapt to diverse communication styles

5. Crisis Leadership

  • Stay Calm: Model composure under pressure
  • Communicate Transparently: Share what you know and what you don't
  • Act Decisively: Balance speed with thoughtfulness
  • Support Your Team: Prioritize wellbeing alongside performance
